Our Mission

Although a city rich in history and beauty, Patna faces significant challenges. India's nearly 80 million stray animals endure harsh climates and scarce resources, compounded by a cultural perception that often sees them as pests or dangers. We're striving to rekindle compassion in Patna's approach to stray animals, alleviating the pressure on India's overwhelmed shelter system through funding, organizational support, education, and more.

Rescue and Sterilization:

We actively rescue and sterilize stray dogs, controlling the population and improving their quality of life

Lost and Found:

Reuniting lost pets with their families through our efficient and compassionate lost and found program.

Water Bowl Drive:

Ensuring hydration for stray animals with our community-driven water bowl initiative during the scorching summer months.

Virtual Adoption:

is a concept where individuals or families support and care for a dog in need without physically owning or residing with the animal.
